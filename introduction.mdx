# Welcome to Hubzz

Hubzz is a **hyper-casual metaverse** designed for mainstream users, blending social media, virtual worlds, and blockchain technology into an accessible platform where digital communities thrive.

<Callout type="info">
  This documentation will help you navigate Hubzz's features and opportunities based on how you plan to use the platform.
</Callout>

## What Makes Hubzz Different?

Hubzz offers a unique blend of accessibility and depth:

- **Drop in, Drop out** — Use the platform for minutes or hours, based on your schedule
- **Multi-device compatibility** — Access via mobile, desktop, or VR without specialized hardware
- **Community-driven experiences** — Participate in events and communities that matter to you
- **True digital ownership** — Own virtual properties, wearables, and collectibles through blockchain integration
- **Creator empowerment** — Monetize your presence through multiple revenue streams
