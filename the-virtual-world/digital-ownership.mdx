## True Digital Ownership

<Tip>
Our "Blockchain-on-the-Backend" approach ensures users get the benefits of true digital ownership without needing technical expertise.
</Tip>

Hubzz provides genuine ownership of digital assets through a hybrid approach that combines the best of centralized and decentralized systems:

  </Card>
