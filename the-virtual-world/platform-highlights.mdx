## Adult-Focused Design

<Tip>
Unlike most virtual world platforms that target children or teenagers, Hubzz is specifically designed for adult users.
</Tip>

This focus influences every aspect of our platform:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Content Standards">
    - Mature (but not explicit) content is permitted
    - Sophisticated themes and discussions
    - Higher-quality creative content
    - More nuanced community guidelines
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Economic Opportunity">
    - Real economic opportunities for creators
    - Sustainable business models for communities
    - Genuine digital ownership with value
    - Multiple monetization options
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="UI/UX Considerations">
    - Less gamified, more sophisticated interface
    - Focus on efficiency and usability
    - Respect for user's time and attention
    - Preference for quality over quantity
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Management">
    - More self-governance capabilities
    - Sophisticated moderation tools
    - Nuanced content policies
    - Focus on authentic engagement
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
